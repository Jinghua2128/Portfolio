About Me

I am a Year 3 Diploma student in Immersive Media at Ngee Ann Polytechnic. My interests include game design, interactive media, UI/UX, motion graphics, video editing, and 3D art.

I enjoy exploring how creativity and technology can work together to make digital experiences more meaningful. Whether I am designing a game system, editing a video, planning motion, or building a website, I try to make the work clear and purposeful.

I also have experience in leadership, event planning, cultural activities, and multimedia storytelling. These experiences help me collaborate, organise ideas, and present creative work with confidence.
